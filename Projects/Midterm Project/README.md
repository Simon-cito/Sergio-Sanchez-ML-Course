Midterm Project: ShoppinAI – The Semantic Concierge
Overview
ShoppinAI is a business and retail machine learning application designed to classify consumer goods and optimize procurement based on dietary needs and "edible intent." By analyzing product names and department metadata, the system distinguishes between food and non-food items (e.g., distinguishing "Apple" the fruit from "Apple" the scent) to facilitate more efficient "Cloud Store" logistics and sustainable urban procurement.

The Problem
Traditional retail models are often inefficient "legacy interfaces" that contribute to high energy consumption and traffic congestion. A major technical challenge in automating these stores is Semantic Ambiguity—standard keyword searches often fail to distinguish between edible products and non-edible household goods with similar names.

My Approach
Data Engineering: Processed a massive dataset (utilizing logic scalable to 32 million rows) to classify products into "Edible" vs. "Non-Edible" categories.

Natural Language Processing (NLP): Implemented "Keyword Grounding" to analyze word proximity, ensuring the model uses department context to solve semantic traps.

Algorithm Selection: Conducted a literature review comparing probabilistic ranking to neural networks, ultimately implementing a high-performance classification brain optimized for retail catalogs.

Optimization: Used stratified subsampling and data type casting to ensure the model could run efficiently on standard hardware without losing precision.

Results & Insights
Scalability: The logic successfully navigated over 1 million rows of data, proving that the architecture is ready for full-scale deployment.

Precision: The model demonstrated high accuracy in identifying "Edible Intent," a critical metric for reducing waste in automated grocery systems.

Sustainability Impact: By moving toward a "Cloud Store" model supported by this AI, we can potentially reduce commercial building energy consumption and reclaim urban land currently used for large-scale parking.

"Ranger" Connection
ShoppinAI is the first step toward my goal of building "Expert Aides." In a laboratory or field environment, a robot must be able to distinguish between critical samples and background noise. The NLP and classification techniques I refined here are being directly applied to my "To Green Pastures" project and the Microscope Buddy, allowing my robotic systems to understand complex human intent and environmental context with higher precision.
